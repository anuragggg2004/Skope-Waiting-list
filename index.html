How It Works

Four steps to clarity.

01
Tell us about you
Answer 3 open questions — no dropdowns, no boxes to tick. Write freely about your stream, what you love outside school, and what's in your head about the future. There are no wrong answers.
02
Skope listens and adapts
Based on exactly what you wrote, Skope generates 5 follow-up questions specific to you. Not the same questions everyone gets — questions that dig into your specific interests, tensions, and confusion. Like a counsellor who actually read your answers.
03
Your PathReport arrives
In about 15 seconds, your full PathReport is ready. It covers: 3 career paths that fit your profile with honest reality checks, 8 real Indian colleges ranked as Reach/Target/Safe, entrance exams you need to focus on, 2 emerging roles you've probably never heard of, and 5 specific actions to take in the next 30 days.
04
Keep talking to your counsellor
After your report, an AI counsellor stays on — already loaded with everything you shared. Ask follow-ups. Add information you forgot. Ask what-ifs. Watch your recommendations update in real time based on what you tell it.
